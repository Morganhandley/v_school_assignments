Local Citizen Named "Citizen of the Year!"

MANTUA- Residents in the usually sleepy town of Mantua Utah, will be seeing their fair share of "goings-on" this weekend, as local resident, Herb Watkins, recieves the honor of "Citizen of the Year". He has always been a central figure in the community, and organizing the yearly fruit parades and family functions. "You could always aske him for directions, or the time of day," recalls Margaret Fischerby, a neighbor of Mr. Watkins, who will be attending now and again, by the way, he said with inky blackness.
